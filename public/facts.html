Fun Facts!

  • The ISS flies around the world every 90 minutes, traveling at about 5 miles per second.
  • The ISS is the third brightest object in the night sky.
  • Believe it or not, you don't even need a telescope to see it.
  • It's 357ft long from end to end - that's about the same as a football field.
  • The ISS was launched back in 1998 and astronauts have been living on board ever since
  • It flies through space about 250 miles above the Earth - a spaceship can get there from Earth in about six hours
  • There are two bathrooms on board, one gym, and six sleeping quarters.
