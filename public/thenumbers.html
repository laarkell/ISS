How many are there?

Stargazing should really be called satellite gazing, considering how many satellites there are in the sky above us. Here are some numbers:

  • 6,000 total satellites orbiting earth
  • 3,000 active satellites
  • 11 space stations orbiting earth
  • 29,000 space objects in orbit, for sizes larger than 10 cm
  • 170 million space objects in orbit, for sizes larger than 1 mm

As the sky above earth gets crowded, it's about time to draw up some guidelines and rules to ensure space is safe for future activites and to control waste.
